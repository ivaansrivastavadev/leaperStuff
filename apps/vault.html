About leprVault

leprVault uses multi-layer key derivation to protect your sensitive text. All computation happens entirely inside your browser — nothing leaves your device.

Encryption Layers
1
Long Passcode
Your primary secret, seeded into the key derivation function.
2
24-Word Sequence
A sequence of 24 ordered words expands the key entropy significantly.
3
Range / Offset
A numeric shift applied to every key byte. Higher values increase resistance to brute-force.
4
File ZIP Password Coming soon
An additional passphrase applied to the encrypted file archive.
⚠
Keep your credentials safe
Your passcode, 24 words, and offset value are never stored anywhere. If you lose them, your data cannot be recovered.
✦
Complete privacy
No servers. No analytics. No ads. No cookies. No external network requests. leprVault is fully offline-capable.
