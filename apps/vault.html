About leprVault

leprVault is a secure encryption application that uses multi-layer encryption to protect your sensitive data.

Encryption Layers:

  • Layer 1: Long passcode encryption
  • Layer 2: 24-word sequence encryption
  • Layer 3: Range/offset encryption
  • Layer 4 (Files only): Password-protected ZIP encryption

How It Works:

Your data is encrypted using a combination of these layers, making it extremely difficult to decrypt without all the correct credentials.

Important: Always keep your passcode, 24 words, and range/offset value secure. Without these, your data cannot be recovered.
