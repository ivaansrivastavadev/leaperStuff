About LeaperStuff

LeaperStuff is a personal and creative web space built to share simple, fun, and useful web tools, experiments, and projects. Whether it's a text editor, a no-login chat, or a visualizer – it's all here under one roof.

This site is driven by a love for coding and clean, accessible design. It aims to offer lightweight apps and pages that just work — no logins, no bloat, just quick ideas brought to life.

Projects like ZenCodeEdit and LeaperChatGun are examples of the goal: minimalist tools with purpose.
